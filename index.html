Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI optimization?

AI optimization involves improving website content and structure to be more accessible and comprehensible to Large Language Models (LLMs) and AI systems.

How does this improve my website?

Optimized websites are better understood by AI systems, leading to improved search visibility, better AI-generated summaries, and enhanced user experience.

What are the key improvements made?

Key improvements include adding structured data, meta descriptions, author information, FAQ structures, and enhanced content organization for better AI comprehension.
